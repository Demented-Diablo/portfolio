About Me


Hello World, (originality stonks)
My name is Gavin Sharma and this is my ever-changing and ever-growing portfolio.

As a kid, I was always drawn to technology. I vividly remember the excitement of
encountering my first smartphone at the age of nine when my dad brought it home.
The sheer variety of games and applications captivated me instantly.
Curiosity sparked within me as I began to inquire: How are these games created?
What makes this device so fascinating? Why hadn't we seen something like this before?
Although I hadn't pondered it deeply at the time, my fascination with technology grew exponentially,
igniting a passion that would shape my future.

Throughout my school years, discussions about career paths were distant whispers until the looming
reality of graduation approached. It was during my 11th grade that I was introduced to Python.
Memories of my childhood dreams and the allure of the coding world flooded back, reminding me of the
boundless possibilities that lay within the realm of software development.
It was then that I realized what I wanted to pursue in life:
the opportunity to build extraordinary creations that could impact lives and inspire innovation.

With newfound clarity, I embarked on my journey towards fulfilling my dreams. I enrolled
at Dalhousie University in Canada, eager to immerse myself in the world of computer science
and transform my aspirations into reality. Each day is a step closer to realizing my goal
of creating innovative solutions and contributing to the ever-evolving landscape of technology.

Education

I was introduced to computer science in my highschool at Lawrence Public School
